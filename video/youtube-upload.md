## Description

```
RegShift turns a regulation into reviewable engineering work. It parses the EU AI Act into atomic obligations, maps them onto a real codebase, finds the gaps, and compiles change proposals with diffs — while a human approves every step. Local-first by design: the pipeline runs on a MacBook with qwen2.5-coder:7b via Ollama, so your code never leaves your machine. Zero cloud calls.

This demo is a real run, no mocks: EU AI Act (Regulation (EU) 2024/1689) reviewed against LibreChat — 267 files scanned, 1,139 code chunks, 8 articles distilled into 24 verbatim-sourced obligations, 24 findings, 17 gaps, and 19 change proposals. You'll see the obligations register, the impact map pinning Art. 50(1) to the exact controllers it governs, a proposal approved with a recorded comment, and a one-click regulator-ready audit report.

Live console: https://regshift.vercel.app
Code: https://github.com/stetang98/regshift
Built solo with Claude Code for the UK AI Agent Hackathon EP5 × Conduct.
```
